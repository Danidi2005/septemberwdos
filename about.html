Welcome To Unity Hospital Branches

At Unity Hospital, we are committed to providing top-quality healthcare to communities across Sri Lanka.
With branches in Matara and Kandy, we ensure that our patients have access to comprehensive medical services close to home.
Both of our branches are equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and staffed by experienced healthcare professionals dedicated to your well-being.
Whether you need emergency care, specialized treatments, or routine medical services, our Matara and Kandy branches are here to serve you with the excellence that Unity Hospital is known for.

Matara Unity hospital

Located in the heart of Matara, our Unity Hospital branch is dedicated to providing exceptional
healthcare services to the southern region of Sri Lanka. With state-of-the-art medical facilities,
a highly skilled team of doctors and staff, and 24/7 emergency services, we are committed
to offering personalized care in a compassionate environment. Whether it’s routine check-ups,
specialized treatments, or urgent medical attention, our Matara branch is here to ensure your health is always our priority.

Kandy Unity Hospital

Nestled in the scenic city of Kandy, our Unity Hospital branch brings top-tier medical care to the central region of Sri Lanka. Equipped with modern
technology and a dedicated team of healthcare professionals, we provide a wide range of medical services from preventive healthcare to advanced treatments.
With a focus on patient-centered care and a commitment to excellence,
our Kandy branch ensures that every patient receives the best possible treatment in a
welcoming and caring environment.
