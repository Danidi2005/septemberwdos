Services we provide

At our hospital, we are committed to delivering comprehensive and high-quality healthcare services tailored to meet the unique needs of every patient.
Our team of highly trained professionals and state-of-the-art facilities ensure that you receive the best care available.
Below is an overview of some of the key services we offer:

Accident and Emergency
Our Accident and Emergency department operates 24/7, providing immediate care for critical and life-threatening conditions. With a highly trained medical team and state-of-the-art facilities, we are equipped to handle emergencies efficiently and with the utmost care.

Healthcare Services
Our Healthcare Services cover a wide range of medical needs, from routine check-ups to specialized treatments. With a focus on preventive care and overall well-being, we ensure that every patient receives personalized and comprehensive medical attention.

Laboratory
Our advanced laboratory provides precise and timely diagnostic services to support accurate treatment plans. With modern equipment and experienced staff, we ensure reliable test results for a wide range of medical conditions.

Facilities & Ameniti
At Unity Hospital, we provide a range of facilities to ensure your comfort during your visit. Our amenities include free Wi-Fi, ample parking, on-site cafeterias, and comfortable patient rooms, all designed to make your stay as convenient and pleasant as possible.
